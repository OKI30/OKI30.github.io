Fabric printing

Fabric printing is the process of printing patterns on fabrics. Type printing method: version printing, silk screen printing, roller rotary screen printing, transfer printing and multi-color pour dye printing methods, including silk screen printing is derived from the type version of the printed type (including print and zinc plate), start by manual operation, gradually towards the semi-automatic and fully automatic, then peace network development to cylinder type.

Paint direct printing

Direct printing is the printing color paste which is printed directly on the fabric. It is the simplest and most popular printing process. Coating direct printing process is generally refers to the white or light color fabric printing, it is easy to match color, simple process, after printing by baking processing, applicable to all kinds of fiber textiles. Coating direct printing process can be divided into Akramine F binder according to the binder often used. There are three direct printing processes of acrylate adhesive butadiene emulsion and chitin adhesive.

Discharge printing

Discharge printing, also known as carving printing, refers to the process of using discharge agent to print on colored fabric to destroy the dyed pigment and obtain various patterns and patterns. A discharge agent is a chemical that dulls the base of a stain. Hanging Baiji, stannous chloride, etc. This method can be divided into patterns for white (white) and patterns for colored (printing) two. The latter dyeing method makes the pattern of the fabric fine, but the process is complex and the cost is high.

Silk burnt-out

After printing and chemical processing, the part of the natural fiber that is not acid-resistant will be decomposed, and the part of the chemical fiber that is acid-resistant will be exposed, forming a crystal and transparent pattern. Its special style makes the product have a high-grade and gorgeous feeling. Burnt-out products are not only gorgeous in appearance, but also of excellent quality, featuring wear-resisting, strong and high, good washing, quick-drying and non-ironing performance, which are welcomed by domestic and foreign markets. The research and application of burnt-out technology is popular in textile printing and dyeing industry.

Wool printing

Printed wool fabrics to thin plain fabrics or tulle fabrics, mostly used to make women's shirts, dresses, scarves and skirts. Wool fabric printing in addition to the use of direct printing, there are printing, printing and transfer printing. Wool blended fabric printing also accounts for a certain proportion, wool fabric embossing finishing is also popular, mainly used for woolen sweaters.

DThe printed velveteen

Diamond printing takes advantage of the characteristics of silk-screen printing on velvety to obtain a special shining effect, which can give off natural diamond-like light under sunlight.

Foaming printing

There are many kinds of fabric special printing, foaming printing is one of them. Foaming printing is characterized by: strong three-dimensional sense, similar to artificial embroidery, soft feel, wear-resisting, washable, good fastness, full of elasticity, and can be widely used in tablecloth, curtain, tea towel, handkerchief, pillow towel, clothing, tourism souvenirs and other aspects. It not only can be used in cotton, hemp, silk and other natural fiber fabrics, but also can be used in man-made fiber fabrics.

Flocking printing

Electrostatic flocking is a new technology emerging in textile industry. It is a kind of product that USES high voltage electrostatic field to plant short fiber on grey cloth. Flocking product technology is simple, three-dimensional strong, low cost, so it is widely used in rubber, plastic, artificial leather, decorative products, especially for small batch of tourism products show its incomparable superiority. The use of flocking patterns on shoes and hats, children's clothing, trademarks and clothing will make their style and style unique.

Transfer printing

Transfer printing is a printing process which relies on the sublimation of dyes and the diffusion and affinity of dye vapor to fibers to achieve the coloring effect. Transfer printing is a practical method of printing and dyeing in anhydrous processing. In addition to not using water, another major feature is the small deformation of the paper, so that the printing can be fine multi-level patterns and photography pictures. Transfer the pattern picture to the cloth realistically. Its effect is sometimes better than general anti - discharge printing.

Special printing mode

In addition to the above ten common printing methods, there are also some special printing methods, such as: anodized transfer printing, photoluminescence printing, fluorescent printing, gold and silver powder printing, paint foam printing, color changing printing, fragrance printing, diamond printing and pearl printing, etc.
